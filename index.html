隐私政策

Privacy Policy

最后更新 · Last Updated
2026年4月
📦 我们不收集任何数据 · We collect no data
ToDoIsLand 不收集、不传输、不存储任何用户个人信息。所有数据（待办、便笺、番茄钟记录、偏好设置）均仅保存在您的本机设备上，不会上传至任何服务器。
ToDoIsLand does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. All data (todos, notes, Pomodoro records, preferences) is stored locally on your device only and is never sent to any server.
🔒 本地存储 · Local Storage Only
App 数据通过 macOS 标准机制（UserDefaults）存储在本机，卸载 App 后数据将被系统清除。我们无法访问这些数据。
App data is stored locally using standard macOS mechanisms (UserDefaults). Uninstalling the app removes all data. We have no access to this data.
🔔 系统权限 · System Permissions
App 仅在用户主动授权时请求以下权限，且仅用于对应功能：
  • 辅助功能 — 用于检测鼠标悬停刘海区域、点击外部自动收起
  • 通知 — 用于待办提醒推送（可选，仅用户开启时生效）
The app only requests the following permissions when explicitly granted by the user, used solely for the stated purpose:
  • Accessibility — to detect notch hover and auto-collapse on outside click
  • Notifications — for todo reminders (optional, only when enabled by user)
📡 第三方服务 · Third-party Services
App 内不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK。
No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are integrated into the app.
